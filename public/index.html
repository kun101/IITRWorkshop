About the Workshop

IIT Ropar invites you to a 4 day enthralling workshop infused with peer learning and random walks.

The workshop will be done under the mentorship of Dr. Sudarshan Iyengar, HOD, Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Ropar with the help of volunteers chosen by him.

The workshop aims to make sure that participants get indulged in discussions with their peers and compute problems in a fun way. In the process, they will also get a chance to interact with PhD Scholars from IIT Ropar and get the much needed exposure as a student.

Accommodation

The dates for the workshop are 17th July to 20th July. All the participants must reach the campus by the evening of 16th July.
Accommodation and food services will be given by IIT Ropar but the participants must pay the charges involved.
